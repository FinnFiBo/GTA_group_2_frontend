Recreational Index

With this app, you can evaluate your walking route based on its potential for relaxation. An 'RI' of 0 is the worst possible value and an 'RI' of 100 is the best. The 'Trip RI' is calculated from various datasets, including noise pollution, air pollution and the amount of trees nearby. Currently, this app is only functional for the city of Zürich! Press 'Start' to begin your trip and press 'End' to finish it. 'My trips' shows you all your trips and the Mean RI of them. The 'City RI' indicates the current mean RI of all user trips.
